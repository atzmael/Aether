the context

School project made in 2019 based on the theme " Beauty / Digital Beauty ".

The obvious common point between Beauty ( what we learn to appreciate / what subjectively moves us ) and digital beauty ( Who makes the rules ? How is digital implied ? etc.. ) is the Human Being.

What is beautiful and very specific to him is the emotions he feels. Neuroscientists and psychologists say that emotions influence the way we see the world, hence its beauty. Therefore, we focused on emotions and how they can blur and intensify at the same time our unique perception.

Aetherâ€™s aim is to make one think about his emotions and try to understand its system. We chose to focus on 4 of them : joy, sadness, fear and anger. We looked up to how we can visually translate emotions and we developed a world with its own rules for each one. The user is invited to interact as much as possible with the environment. Easter eggs are waiting for the most curious / daring ones.
